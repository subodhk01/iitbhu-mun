FAQs

Q. What is Model United Nations?

Model United Nations ( MUN) is an academic simulation of the United Nations that aims to educate and encourage participants to discuss about major issues concerning the world, topics in international brelations , diplomacy and teh United Nations agenda.

Q. How many committees are there in IITBHU MUN 2019 and What are they?

There are 7 committees in this year's conference :

1.Special political and decolonization committee (SPECPOL)

2.United Nations Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC)

3.Lok Sabha

4.All India Political Parties Meet (AIPPM)

5.United Nations Human Rights Council (UNHRC)

6.United Nations Commission on the Status of Woemen (UNCSW)

7.North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO)

Q. What are the dates of IITBHU MUN?

IITBHU MUN is a three day conference and will be held from 30th of August to 1st of September.

Q. When do I get an allotment?

Allotment will be done within a few days. To confirm your allotment, you'll have to pay the delegate fees within 1 week, failing which, your allotment will be cancelled.

Q. How do I travel to the venue?

If you take accommodation with us, we will get you to the conference and back to the hotel in our vehicle. Others can reach Lecture Theatre–3, IITBHU on your own.

Q. Is previous experience required to apply as a delegate?

To apply as a delegate no previous experience is required.
