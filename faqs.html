FAQs

Q. What is Model United Nations?

Model United Nations (MUN) is an academic simulation of the United Nations that aims to educate and encourage participants to discuss major issues concerning the world, topics in international relations, diplomacy and the United Nations agenda.

Q. How many committees are there in IITBHU MUN 2021 and What are they?

There are 6 committees in this year's conference :

1.International Monetary Fund(IMF)

2.United Nations Security Council (UNSC)

3.Lok Sabha

4.Social, Humanitarian and Cultural Committee(SOCHUM)

5.United Nations Commission on Science and Technology for Development(UNCSTD)

6.UN Women

Q. What are the dates of IITBHU MUN?

IITBHU MUN is a three day conference and will be
held from 16th of April to 18th of April.

Q. When do I get an allotment?

Allotment will be done within a few days. To confirm your
allotment, you'll have to pay the delegate fees within
1 week, failing which, your allotment will be cancelled.

Q. What will be the manner of conduction of
the conference?

Due to the COVID-19 crisis, the MUN conference this year
will be conducted in an online manner. The platform for the
MUN conference this year will be Zoom.

Q. Is previous experience required
to apply as a delegate?

To apply as a delegate no previous experience is required.
