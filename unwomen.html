UN Women

United Nations Women

UN Women supports the UN Member States, as they set global standards for achieving gender equality and works with governments and civil society to design laws, policies, programmes and services needed to ensure that the measures are effectively implemented and truly benefit women. The General Assembly, ECOSOC, and the CSW constitute the governance structure that sets forth its normative policy guiding principles, while the General Assembly, ECOSOC and its Executive Board are in charge of its operational policy guidelines.
