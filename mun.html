"It's not just about ideas, it's about making ideas happen."

We welcome you all to one of India's biggest MUN conferences- IITBHU MUN, a confluence of fierce debaters and suave negotiators.

We are thrilled to invite you to the 2021 online edition of IITBHU Model United Nations conference. It would be a privilege to host young enthusiastic minds
and engage in spirited debates on burning issues facing the world. We pride ourselves in providing you one of the
best comprehensive delegate experiences possible.

This 16th April - 18th April, come and join this spree of debating and interaction by choosing from 6 different committes with pressing agendas.
