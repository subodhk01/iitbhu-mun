ABOUT US

"It's not just about ideas, it's about making ideas happen."

We welcome you all to the oldest surviving city in the world, with ghats drowned in peace and tranquility right in the middle of countless temples. IITBHU MUN, a confluence of fierce debaters and suave negotiators. IITBHU MUN has come a long way since it's inception in 2012, we have grown to become one of the most sought-after conferences in the country.

We are thrilled to invite you to the 2019 edition of IITBHU model United Nations conference. It would be a privilege to host you all this August at IIT (BHU) Varanasi campus and invite the young and enthusiastic minds to cater to the need to recognize leaders and thinkers globally. We pride ourselves in providing you one of the best comprehensive delegate experience possible.

This 30th August – 1st September 2019, come and join this spree of debating and interaction by choosing from 7 different committes with pressing agendas.
